# Play a Song

Short answer: choose a song, choose an arrangement if prompted, tune if needed, and press Play.

## Steps

1. Open the Songs screen.
2. Select a song card.
3. Choose the arrangement you want to play.
4. Confirm tuning if the song requires it.
5. Press Play.
6. Play along with the highway, tab, or active plugin view.
7. Review the result when the song ends.

## What You Should See

- A player view loads.
- The selected arrangement appears.
- Playback controls are available.
- Plugins may add additional views or panels.

## Common Problems

| Problem | Try This |
|---|---|
| Wrong arrangement opens | Return to the song and choose another arrangement. |
| No scoring | Confirm Note Detection is enabled and calibrated. |
| Tuner opens first | Tune or close the tuner, depending on your settings. |
